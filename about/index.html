GENORITHM, a simple web-based generative art maker, was developed in partial fulfillment of the requirements for MATH 10 during the A.Y. 2024–2025 at the University of the Philippines Los Baños. This project aims to allow users to experiment with algorithmically driven art forms in an interactive environment. It enables real-time customization and exploration, demonstrating how mathematical patterns and creative code can be used to produce art that is both structured and expressive. This aligns with the course theme, "Exploring Intersections of Mathematics and the Arts Through Creative Expressions", by transforming abstract mathematical concepts into tangible, interactive artworks—revealing the creative potential of math beyond problem-solving.
